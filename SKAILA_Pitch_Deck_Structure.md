## Slide 2: The Problem (The "Pain")
- **Visual:** A split screen showing a stressed teacher with piles of paper vs. a bored student looking at a clunky 1990s interface (ClasseViva/Axios).

    3.  **Passive:** Zero insights. Data goes in, nothing comes out.

- **Visual:** Video montage (5 sec) of SKAJLA's "SuperApp" flow: Login -> Check Grades -> Chat with Class -> Ask AI Coach a question.

    - **AI-Native:** Intelligent coaching, not just data entry.
    - **Engaging:** Gamified learning that students actually *want* to use.
