## 3. Product & Technology

SKAJLA is built on a "Production-Grade" architecture designed for scale and security.

**Core Technology Stack:**

- **Backend**: Python/Flask (Microservices ready)
- **Database**: PostgreSQL (Neon Tech) + Redis (Caching/Session)
- **Infrastructure**: Dockerized Deployment on Hetzner Cloud
- **Real-Time**: Socket.IO for instant messaging and presence
- **AI Core**: Proprietary "Skaila Brain" + OpenAI Integration

**Key Differentiators:**

- **The "SuperApp" Approach**: One login for everything (Grades + Chat + AI + Jobs).
- **AI Sovereignty**: Localized AI models trained on Italian curriculum requirements.
- **Privacy First**: Multi-tenant architecture ensuring strict GDPR compliance per school.
